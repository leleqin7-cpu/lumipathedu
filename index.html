What You Need

Application Requirements

Know what top universities expect — academically and beyond.

TOP 10 & Ivy League

Academic Benchmarks
GPA 3.8+ · TOEFL 110+ · SAT 1540+ / ACT 34+
  • International competition awards (IMO, IOI, ISEF, REGENERON STS, USAMO)
  • Published papers in high-quality academic journals or conferences (IEEE, NATURE)
  • Highly selective summer programs (RSI, SSHI, TASP, SUMAC, ROSS, PROMYS)
  • Founder or core leader of multiple communities or organizations
  • Drove real-world change (policy, nonprofit, tech product, social enterprise)
  • Distinctive cultural background, family story, or personal growth narrative

TOP 30

Academic Benchmarks
GPA 3.7+ · TOEFL 108+ · SAT 1500+ / ACT 32+
  • National or state-level academic competitions
  • Independent research projects with mentor guidance or published results
  • Founded or led 1 distinctive activity (nonprofit, app, community project)
  • Core roles in other activities demonstrating sustained impact
  • Deep subject-matter interest with a demonstrable exploration process

TOP 50

Academic Benchmarks
GPA 3.5+ · TOEFL 100+
  • Active participation in online courses (Coursera, EdX, Audit University Courses)
  • Clear academic interest area with demonstrated exploration process
  • Active involvement in school and community clubs and volunteer service
  • Sustained commitment in at least one area (music, sports, debate, STEM, writing)

Oxford / Cambridge

Academic Benchmarks
IELTS 7.5+ · 3 A-Levels (A*A*A minimum)
  • Some subjects require additional tests (ESTA, TMUA, etc.)
  • Deep subject passion demonstrated in personal statement
  • Super-curricular activities beyond standard academics
  • Interview readiness — critical thinking under pressure

Russell Group Universities

Academic Benchmarks
IELTS 7.0–7.5 · 1–2 A-Levels A*
  • Strong subject focus with relevant academic activities
  • Personal statement showing deep subject interest and reading
  • Work experience or research relevant to chosen field

Top UK Universities

Academic Benchmarks
IELTS 6.5–7.0 · 2–3 A-Levels
  • Consistent academic performance across subjects
  • Relevant extracurriculars aligned with major choice
  • Compelling personal statement with authentic voice

HKU / CUHK / HKUST

Academic Benchmarks
IELTS 7.0+ · 2–3 A-Levels
  • Deep subject insight, especially for STEM programs
  • Strong letters of recommendation from relevant teachers
  • Relevant research or academic projects

National University of Singapore

Academic Benchmarks
IELTS 7.0–7.5 · TOEFL 110+ · GPA 3.8+
  • Very high academic standards across all subjects
  • Subject olympiad participation recommended
  • Strong all-round academic and extracurricular profile

PolyU / CityU HK

Academic Benchmarks
IELTS 6.5–7.0 · GPA 3.5+
  • Strong academic base with subject relevance
  • Portfolio or creative work required for design programs
  • Interview component for select programs
